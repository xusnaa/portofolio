What is iot?

The term "IoT" stands for the "Internet of Things." It refers to the network of physical devices, vehicles, appliances, and other objects embedded with sensors, software, and connectivity to exchange data with other systems and devices over the internet. In simple terms, IoT involves connecting everyday objects to the internet, allowing them to send and receive data.

Project

An IoT Smart Plant Monitoring System is a technological solution designed to enhance plant care and cultivation through the integration of sensors, connectivity, and data analytics.

Benifits of IOT

  • Increases efficiency by automating routine tasks and improving productivity.
  • Enables real time monitoring and tracking of assets or conditions in a physical environment.
  • Provides valuable insights that can be used to make better decisions.
  • Wearable IoT devices and healthcare applications enable continuous health monitoring and early detection of health issues.
  • IoT facilitates the development of new products and services with innovative features.

Disadvantages of iot

  • Security risks: IoT devices are vulnerable to cyber attacks, which can lead to loss of sensitive information.
  • The cost of IoT devices can be high, which may limit accessibility for some businesses or individuals.
  • Downtime: IoT systems may experience downtime due to technical issues, maintenance, or system failures, impacting the reliability of connected services.
  • Data Privacy: The vast amount of data collected by IoT devices raises concerns about individual privacy
